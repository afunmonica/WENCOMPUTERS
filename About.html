Overview of Wen Computers

Can-West Limited is a global wholesale distributor IT equipment, specialising in desktop PC’s, laptops, printers, servers, security systems, workstations and etc. Serving domestic GH, UAE and worldwide export markets since 1997, Can-West LTD provides access to quantities of IT equipment from HP, Dell, Acer, APC by Schneider Electric, Cisco, Lenovo, SONY and other major brand manufacturers.Our industry leading infrastructure in wholesale distribution and logistics combines with an experienced, knowledgeable and personable sales team who strive to build lasting customer relationships.Atlantic Computers & Electronics, aims at helping clients maximize return on IT investments by supporting the full life-cycle of your key IT initiatives, from assessment and design to deployment and on-going management.We can help your employees collaborate more efficiently with a Unified communications solution. Our experienced solution architects and engineers work with you and our industry-leading partners to give you solutions and services across the entire IT life-cycle. Free up your team to focus on what really matters: better business outcomes.
